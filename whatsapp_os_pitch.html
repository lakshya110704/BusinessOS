Go-to-Market — Vertical First

We do not go horizontal. We go impossibly deep into pharma distribution in Maharashtra first — 6,000 distributors in dense geographic clusters who all know each other.

Why pharma distributors
Structured vocabulary (batch numbers, SKUs, expiry), regulatory pressure creating willingness to pay, tight community networks where one conversion triggers ten referrals, and order patterns that make AI accuracy near-perfect.
Distribution without a sales team
CA partnerships (every SMB trusts their accountant), trade association MOUs, and the anchor supplier strategy — onboard the biggest supplier in a cluster and their 200 buyers have to follow. Word of mouth does the rest.
